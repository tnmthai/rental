## What to Look For in a Flat

### Must-Check Items
- **Bond** — legally max 4 weeks rent
- **Bills** — is power/internet included?
- **Heating** — NZ winters are cold, especially South Island
- **Insulation** — check ceiling and underfloor
- **Flatmates** — meet them before committing
- **Location** — check bus routes, commute time

### Red Flags 🚩
- No written tenancy agreement
- Asking for cash only, no receipts
- Pressure to decide immediately
- Landlord won't show property
- Unusually cheap rent (could be scam)

## Flatmate Agreement Tips

1. **Discuss expectations** — cleaning, guests, noise, shared food
2. **Split bills fairly** — use Splitwise or similar app
3. **Written agreement** — even informal, just write it down
4. **Emergency contacts** — know who to call
5. **Exit plan** — what happens if someone wants to leave?
