## Budget Tips for Students

1. **Flat with others** — shared houses are 30–50% cheaper than studios
2. **Look for "bills included"** — winter power bills in NZ can be $200+/month
3. **Check bus routes** — living 15 min from campus can save $100/week
4. **Furnished saves money** — buying furniture for 1 year isn't worth it
5. **Apply early** — student flats fill up fast in Jan–Feb
