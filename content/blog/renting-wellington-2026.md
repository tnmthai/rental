# Renting in Wellington — 2026 Guide

Wellington is compact, creative, and competitive. Here's how to find your perfect flat.

## Why Wellington?
- **Walkable city** — most things within 20 min walk
- **Café culture** — more coffee shops per capita than NYC
- **Tech hub** — growing startup scene
- **Student city** — Victoria University brings young energy

## Best Suburbs for Renters

### Newtown & Berhampore
- **Rent:** $200–$300/week
- **Near:** Hospital, Victoria (Te Aro campus)
- **Vibe:** Diverse, affordable, great food

### Aro Valley & Highbury
- **Rent:** $220–$320/week
- **Near:** CBD, Victoria (Kelburn)
- **Vibe:** Hipster, hilly, community feel

### Thorndon & Pipitea
- **Rent:** $240–$350/week
- **Near:** Parliament, Victoria (Pipitea campus)
- **Vibe:** Historic, professional, quiet

### Kilbirnie & Lyall Bay
- **Rent:** $200–$280/week
- **Near:** Airport, beach
- **Vibe:** Coastal, relaxed, good for surfers

### Johnsonville & Newlands
- **Rent:** $180–$260/week
- **Near:** Northern suburbs, bus routes
- **Vibe:** Suburban, affordable, family-friendly

## Tips for Renting in Wellington

1. **Wind factor** — Wellington is windy, check windows and heating
2. **Hills** — some suburbs are very steep, check your commute
3. **Act fast** — Wellington rental market moves quickly
4. **Bus passes** — Snapper card gives discounts
5. **Flatting is the norm** — most young professionals share
