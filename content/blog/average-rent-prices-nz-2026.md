# Average Rent Prices in New Zealand 2026

Understanding rental prices helps you budget effectively. Here's a breakdown by city:

## Auckland
- **Room in a flat**: $200-$350/week
- **Studio apartment**: $350-$500/week
- **1-bedroom apartment**: $400-$600/week

## Wellington
- **Room in a flat**: $180-$300/week
- **Studio apartment**: $300-$450/week
- **1-bedroom apartment**: $350-$550/week

## Christchurch
- **Room in a flat**: $150-$250/week
- **Studio apartment**: $250-$400/week
- **1-bedroom apartment**: $300-$450/week

## Hamilton
- **Room in a flat**: $140-$220/week
- **Studio apartment**: $220-$350/week

## Dunedin
- **Room in a flat**: $120-$200/week
- **Studio apartment**: $200-$320/week

## Tips to Save on Rent
1. **Flat with others** — Splitting costs is the biggest saver
2. **Look slightly further from CBD** — 15min commute can save $50+/week
3. **Bills included** — Look for all-inclusive deals to avoid surprise costs
4. **Negotiate** — Especially for longer lease terms
