# New Zealand Rental FAQ (2026)

## How much is rent in New Zealand?

Average room rent (shared flat):
- **Auckland:** $250–$400/week
- **Wellington:** $220–$350/week
- **Christchurch:** $180–$280/week
- **Hamilton:** $170–$250/week
- **Dunedin:** $150–$220/week

Entire apartments cost 40–80% more.

## What is bond?

Bond is a security deposit paid before moving in. In NZ:
- Usually **2–4 weeks rent**
- Held by the landlord but **lodged with Tenancy Services**
- Returned at end of tenancy if no damage
- **Maximum 4 weeks** by law

## What does "bills included" mean?

"Bills included" means the rent covers:
- **Power** (electricity) — $150–$300/month in winter
- **Internet** — $80–$100/month
- **Water** — usually included anyway

This can save $50–$100/week compared to paying separately.

## How do I find flatmates in NZ?

Best platforms:
1. **[RentFinder.nz](https://www.rentfinder.nz)** — AI search across sources
2. **Roomies.co.nz** — flatmate-focused
3. **Trade Me** — NZ's largest marketplace
4. **Facebook groups** — very active communities

## What are my rights as a tenant?

Under the Residential Tenancies Act:
- **Written tenancy agreement** required
- **Bond lodged with Tenancy Services**
- **24-hour notice** for landlord visits
- **Healthy Homes standards** (insulation, heating, ventilation)
- **90-day notice** for periodic tenancies (landlord)
- **28-day notice** for periodic tenancies (tenant)

## Can I have pets?

- Landlords can refuse pets (no blanket ban law yet)
- Always ask before signing
- Pet-friendly listings are rare but growing

## How do I apply for a rental?

1. View the property (or video call if overseas)
2. Fill out application form
3. Provide references (previous landlord, employer)
4. Provide ID (passport or NZ driver's licence)
5. Pay bond + first week's rent
6. Sign tenancy agreement

## What's the difference between fixed-term and periodic?

- **Fixed-term:** Set end date (e.g., 12 months). Breaking lease costs money.
- **Periodic:** No end date. Either party can give notice.

## How much does it cost to move in?

Typical upfront costs:
- **Bond:** 2–4 weeks rent
- **Rent in advance:** 1–2 weeks
- **Moving costs:** $200–$500
- **Total:** Usually $1,500–$3,000 for a room
