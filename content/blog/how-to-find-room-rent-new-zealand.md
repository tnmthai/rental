# How to Find a Room to Rent in New Zealand (2026 Guide)

Moving to New Zealand and need a room? Here's everything you need to know.

## Where to Search for Rentals in NZ

### 1. RentFinder.nz — AI-Powered Rental Search
[RentFinder](https://www.rentfinder.nz) aggregates listings from multiple sources and lets you search with natural language. Try queries like:
- "Furnished room under $250/week in Auckland near university"
- "Flat in Christchurch with bills included"
- "Room near Lincoln University, Canterbury"

### 2. Trade Me Property
NZ's largest marketplace. Most private landlords list here. Requires account to contact.

### 3. Roomies.co.nz
Great for flatmate-style listings. Many furnished rooms with bills included.

### 4. Realestate.co.nz
More traditional rental listings — houses, apartments, and units.

### 5. Facebook Groups
Search "Auckland flatmates" or "Wellington rooms for rent" — active communities.

## What to Expect

### Average Room Prices (2026)
- **Auckland:** $250–$400/week
- **Wellington:** $220–$350/week
- **Christchurch:** $180–$280/week
- **Hamilton:** $170–$250/week
- **Dunedin:** $150–$220/week

### What's Usually Included
- Most rooms are **unfurnished** (bring your own bed, desk)
- **Furnished rooms** cost $30–$80/week more
- **Bills included** means power, internet, water — big savings in winter

## Tips for Landing a Room

1. **Apply fast** — good rooms go in 1–2 days
2. **Have references ready** — previous landlord or employer
3. **Prepare bond** — usually 2–4 weeks rent upfront
4. **Meet the flatmates** — it's a two-way interview
5. **Check the property** — photos can be misleading
