# Community Posting Kit

Purpose: drive first cohorts of renters to https://www.rentfinder.nz by sharing real prompts/results in the student housing communities they already trust.

## Weekly cadence (repeatable)
| Day | Channel | Angle | CTA | Asset |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Mon | FB Group: "NZ Student Flatting" | "Skip TradeMe filters" story + screenshot | "Try your own natural-language search" | 20s gif / static mock (see hero demo) |
| Tue | Reddit r/newzealand + r/UniOfAuckland | Prompt breakdown (how to type) | "Drop your wishlist, I’ll reply with the AI summary" | Text post + 3 bullet list |
| Wed | Discord: Uni-specific housing channels | Proactive offer to run searches for them live | "DM me your criteria" | Short Loom link when available |
| Thu | FB Group: each uni (AUT, UoA, UC, Otago) | Case study: "found 10 Lincoln rooms under $250" | Link + comment thread answering prompts | screenshot |
| Sat | LinkedIn personal post | Product story + invite for beta testers | "Reply 'beta'" | carousel (3 slides) |

> Tip: rotate cities/budgets each week to keep posts fresh and avoid spam flags.

## Post templates

### Facebook (English)
```
Stuck scrolling TradeMe + FB Marketplace tabs every weekend? I built RentFinder to skip all that.

Type whatever you’re actually looking for (e.g. "Room under $250 near Lincoln Uni with bills included") and it pulls matching listings + AI highlights in one go.

🧪 Just ran it for Lincoln → 10 results with pricing, bills/furnishing tags, and direct contact links.

Give it a try: https://www.rentfinder.nz — hit Enter to search, Shift+Enter for multi-line prompts. Drop your wishlist in the comments and I’ll run it for you.
```

### Facebook (Vietnamese)
```
Cuối tuần nào cũng lọc TradeMe + Marketplace mệt quá? RentFinder cho bạn gõ đúng nhu cầu ("phòng < $250 gần LU, có nội thất, gồm bills") rồi AI sẽ gom listing đúng ngay lập tức.

Vừa test ở Lincoln: 10 phòng đúng yêu cầu, kèm tag nội thất + bills + link liên hệ.

Dùng thử tại https://www.rentfinder.nz (Enter để tìm, Shift+Enter xuống dòng). Comment nhu cầu của bạn, mình chạy giúp.
```

### Reddit (thread opener)
```
I built a natural-language rental search for NZ students. No filters, just type "Studio for 2 near Otago under $380/wk, cat friendly" and it compiles real listings + AI summaries.

Beta is free: https://www.rentfinder.nz

If you drop your wishlist below I’ll reply with the top matches + source links so everyone benefits.
```

### Discord blurb
```
Need rooms fast? DM me your criteria or try https://www.rentfinder.nz — it understands prompts like "2 rooms near Canterbury, $600 budget, furnished" and spits out ready-to-share matches.
```

## Asset checklist
- Screenshot/gif of search → results (use new hero demo or Loom once recorded).
- Badge overlay: "Type like texting a friend".
- Short URL ready (bit.ly / custom) to avoid FB spam filters.

## Follow-up playbook
1. Monitor comments for 24h; answer with tailored prompt + copy/paste of AI summary.
2. Capture FAQs → add to blog/landing page.
3. Track which communities drive visits (use UTM `?utm_source=fb-group-aut` etc.).
